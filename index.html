Lucky Dube

The man who fought against Apertheid

Here's a timeline of Lucky Dube's life:

  • 1964 - Lucky Dube was born on 3rd August on a small farm in Ermelo, South Africa.
  • 1982 - he joined his cousin’s “Zulu” pop music band, The Love Brothers and worked as a security guard.
  • 1984 - he released his first reggae album, Rastas Never Die.
  • 1985 - His album, Rastas Never Die was banned because of its government-critical lyrics.
  • 1986 - He released Think About The Children which attained platinum status.
  • 1995 - He became signed with Motown.
  • 1996 - He released Serious Reggae Business earning him the “Best Selling African Recording Artist” at the “World Music Awards".
  • 2006 - He released Respect which got him a European release deal with Warner Music.
  • 2007 - Died at the age of 46years.
